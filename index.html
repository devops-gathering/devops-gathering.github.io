About DevOps Gathering

The DevOps Gathering is an international conference located in Bochum - surrounded by the metropolitan area of the Ruhr Valley. This melting pot of past times industrial revolutions is nowadays a hot spot for Germany’s IT industry - a perfect place to discuss the next big evolution of our industry and working culture. Let’s discuss the hot topics inside the DevOps community and start talking about CNCF-Projects, Container Ecosystems, Kubernetes, Microservices, Serverless, Clouds and everything else regarding the DevOps culture to automate all.

The bee42 solutions gmbh is hosting a conference striving for high-quality content and providing a platform for experienced international speakers at the G DATA Campus in the heart of Bochum.

Everyone is invited to join to discuss the hottest topics of the Cloud Native development with its impact on the digital transformation. Join our journey and cooperate on infrastructure technology and innovative solutions to revolutionize the software industry.

Topics

  • CNCF Projects
  • OpenContainer Standards
  • Container Ecosystem (Moby Project, CRI, gVisor, kubevirt, virtual-kubelet, container.d, clearcontainers, fargate)
  • Container Orchestration (Docker Swarm, Kubernetes, Mesos, Rancher …)
  • Container Security(CVE Scaning, Network, RBAC, Policies, Auth)
  • Container Networking (CNI, Calico, Weave, cannel, ...)
  • Building Hybrid Systems (Mixed CPU Architectures, different OS's)
  • Docker and Kubernetes on embedded devices (ARM,...) and IoT devices
  • Container for Developers (Java, Go, Ruby, Javascript, PHP, …)
  • Stateful Container (CSI, Rook, PostgresSql Operator, ...)
  • Provisioning (Helm, Draft, Ansible, terraform, ...)
  • Continuous Integration and Delivery (drone.io, Jenkins, Gitlab/CI, circle.ci, ...)
  • Build good Container Images (Img, buildKit, buildah, Docker CE,...)
  • Linuxkit
  • Loadbalancing (Traefik, kong, HAProxy, Nginx)
  • Kubernetes Cloud Services (GKE, ACE, EKS, DKS, ..) and Federation
  • Build your own OnPremise or Cloud Kubernetes Cluster's
  • Logging & Monitoring (Graylog, ELK, EFK, Prometheus, Grafana, DataDog, Insana, InfluxDB …)
  • Serverless (openFass, kubeless, OpenWhisk, Lambda,..)
  • DevOps und Agile Culture (Principles, Project Stories and Use Cases)

Advisory Board

Peter Rossbach

bee42 solutions gmbh

Peter Rossbach is a infra coder, system architect and coach of numerous web systems and founder of the bee42 solutions gmbh. Peter realized appropriate infrastructure products and offers many training based on the Docker and Kubernetes ecosystem, current web technologies, microservices, Apache Tomcat, Java, NoSQL databases and cloud platforms.

Roland Huß

RedHat

Roland Huss is a Principal Software Engineer at Red Hat and tech-lead for Red Hat Fuse Online, an open source hybrid integration platform running on OpenShift. He has been developing mostly in Java for over twenty years but never forgot his roots as a system administrator. Roland is an active open source contributor, developer of the JMX-HTTP bridge Jolokia and the popular fabric8 Maven plugins for Docker, Kubernetes and OpenShift. He is also co-author of the "Kubernetes Patterns" book. And he loves chilli pepper.

Timo Derstappen

Giantswarm

Timo Derstappen is the CTO and Co-Founder of Giant Swarm. Besides managing and scaling distributed services for the past 15 years he is prepared to move off-the-grid as soon as the time is right.
