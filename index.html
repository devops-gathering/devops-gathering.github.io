DevOps Gathering 2018

19th – 21st February, 2018

Call for Papers

Inspire the people: though the second edition of DevOps Gathering Ruhr hasn’t even started yet, we are preparing the next round.

Dear DevOps Friends and Speakers!

After the first and successful DevOps Gathering community conference we are in the preparation of the second edition of DevOps Gathering Conference in February 2018.

Everyone is invited to join discuss the hottest topics of the DevOps development with its impact on the digital transformation. Join our journey and cooperate on infrastructure technology and innovative solutions to revolutionize the software industry.

We are looking forward to proposals with practical relevance – ideally based on project experiences or real technical inspiration.

As usual, we would like to invite you to submit your proposals for sessions in 2018. We will focus on the following topics, but are always open for surprises with new inspiring talks.

Topics

  • Container Standards & Technologies
  • Container Orchestration: Docker, Swarm, Kubernetes, Mesos, Rancher
  • Container Security
  • Container Networking
  • Container for Microservices
  • Provisioning
  • Docker on Windows
  • Docker and Kubernetes on ARM and IoT devices
  • Container for Developers: Java, Go, Ruby, Javascript, PHP,...
  • Stateful Container
  • Continuous Integration and Delivery
  • Microservice based Front-End Development
  • Gitlab/CI
  • LinuxKit
  • Infrakit
  • Loadbalancing: Traefik, kong, HAProxy, Nginx
  • Logging & Monitoring: Graylog, ELK, Prometheus, Grafana,...
  • Cloud

Deadline for submissions: September 6th, 2017

You can offer us 30 minutes talks or workshops. We are looking forward to your comprehensive feedback as well as your questions, suggestions and discussions.
