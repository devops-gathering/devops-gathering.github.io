Student Ticket Sponsoring

Dear DevOps-Friends and students!
We are happy to have the opportunity to sponsor your attendance for the next DevOps Gathering 2020 :-)
You have the chance to get one sponsored ticket for the conference days!

Want to win one ticket? Then please answer the following questions and send it to: info@devops-gathering.io

Please attach a copy of your valid studentID!

  1. At which university are you studying?
  2. What are you studying?
  3. Why are you the right person, to win one of the tickets?
  4. Do you engage yourself in any community groups, projects or other student activities?
  5. If you have one, provide us with your github account (optional)
  6. If you have one, provide us with your twitter account (optional)

We will announce the Winners on the 17th of February!
